Vegetable Soup

To make a hearty vegetable soup, start by sautéing diced onions, carrots, and celery in a large pot with a bit of olive oil until they begin to soften. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute. Pour in vegetable broth and diced tomatoes, then bring to a boil. Add your favorite vegetables like potatoes, green beans, and peas. Season with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs. Reduce heat and let it simmer until all the vegetables are tender. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley or a dollop of sour cream. Enjoy your delicious and healthy vegetable soup!

prep time

20 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Vegetarian Healthy Soup Comfort Food

instructions

step 1

Sauté Vegetables: In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onions, carrots, and celery, and sauté until they begin to soften. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.

step 2

Add Broth and Vegetables: Pour in vegetable broth and diced tomatoes, then bring to a boil. Add potatoes, green beans, peas, and your favorite vegetables. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.

step 3

Simmer and Serve: Reduce heat and let it simmer until all the vegetables are tender. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley or a dollop of sour cream.

ingredients

1 onion, diced

2 carrots, diced

2 celery stalks, diced

3 cloves garlic, minced

4 cups vegetable broth

1 can diced tomatoes

2 potatoes, diced

1 cup green beans

1 cup peas

Salt and pepper to taste

Herbs (thyme, rosemary, etc.)

tools

Large pot

Wooden spoon

Cutting board

Knife
