Banana Pancakes

To make delicious banana pancakes, start by mashing two ripe bananas in a bowl until smooth. Add one cup of all-purpose flour, one tablespoon of sugar, one teaspoon of baking powder, a pinch of salt, one beaten egg, and one cup of milk. Mix everything together until you get a smooth batter. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil. Pour small ladles of batter onto the skillet and cook each pancake for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Serve warm with a drizzle of maple syrup and a sprinkle of fresh banana slices on top.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Meat breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Prepare the Batter: Mash two ripe bananas in a bowl until smooth. Add one cup of all-purpose flour, one tablespoon of sugar, one teaspoon of baking powder, a pinch of salt, one beaten egg, and one cup of milk. Mix everything together until you get a smooth batter.

step 2

Cook the Pancakes: Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil. Pour small ladles of batter onto the skillet. Cook each pancake for about 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown.

step 3
