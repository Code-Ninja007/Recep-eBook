Carrot Pudding

To make a delightful carrot pudding, start by grating fresh carrots. In a large saucepan, combine the grated carrots with milk and cook on medium heat until the carrots are tender and the milk is absorbed. Add sugar, cardamom, and a pinch of saffron, stirring continuously. Continue cooking until the mixture thickens and achieves a pudding-like consistency. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped nuts and raisins. Serve warm or chilled as a delicious dessert.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Dessert Vegetarian Carrots Sweet

instructions

step 1

Prepare the Carrots: Peel and grate the fresh carrots. Set them aside.

step 2

Cook with Milk: In a large saucepan, combine the grated carrots with milk and cook on medium heat until the carrots are tender and the milk is absorbed.

step 3

Add Sugar and Spices: Add sugar, cardamom, and a pinch of saffron, stirring continuously. Continue cooking until the mixture thickens and achieves a pudding-like consistency.

step 4

Garnish and Serve: Remove from heat and garnish with chopped nuts and raisins. Serve warm or chilled as a delicious dessert.

ingredients

2 cups grated carrots

1 cup milk

1/2 cup sugar

1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

A pinch of saffron

Chopped nuts and raisins for garnish

tools

Grater

Large saucepan

Wooden spoon

Mixing bowl
